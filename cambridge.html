Nishanthini Ganesan

About Me

The English Literature Student

I am reading a BA in English Literature at St. Edmund's College. At present, I am specialising in Postcolonial Literature and Chaucer. I am also writing a dissertation on Lebanese-American author, Khalil Gibran's representations of migration.

The Student Librarian (2017 - 2018)

I was the Student Librarian of my college from February 2017 to February 2018. During this time, I pushed for the diversification of our collection through acquiring more books written by BAME and LGBT+ authors.

The CR Welfare Officer (2018 - 2019)

I was the Welfare Officer of my college from February 2018 to 2019. This was huge challenge, but I've pulled through. As a result, I've also found a strength within me that I never knew I had!

The W1 Rower (2016 - 2018)

I rowed with the St. Edmund's College Women's Boat Club from November 2016 to June 2018. During this time, I took part in countless races and several Bumps campaigns. In addition, I attended many boat club dinners.
